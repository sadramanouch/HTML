  1. Elbows: The shooting elbow must be behind the start of the table while releasing the ball.
  2. Gentlemen's: After redemtion, each player of the losing team will get a trickshot as well.
  3. Death cup: Any cup that's been sunk and not removed; If sunk again, will result to a loss.
  4. Redemtion: After getting all the cups sunked, the losing team will get a shot each to redeam.
  5. Naked mile: If losed a game and not sunk any cup, the person will have to run a mile, NAKED.
  6. Explosion: If the two players sink the same cup in the same turn, the cup and all the touching cups are eliminated.
  7. Island: This rule can be used once during a game. If called and sunk the lonely cup, The biggest island in the formation and all it's cups are eliminated.
  8. Balls back: If both played sink a cup in the same turn, the balls are returned to them.
  9. Air ball: If the defenders call this rule while the first shooter's ball has not yet travelled 2/3 of the table length and the does not hit the table or a cup, the second ball is automatically handed over.
  10. Heating up: If sunk 2 cups in 2 turns in a row, you can call this rule. If sunk a 3rd cup in a row, you will be on fire and you will shoot without interuption until a miss.
  11. Trick shot: Examples are eye's closed, left hand, wall shot, bounce shot, downtown, paddle shot, etc...
  12. Party foul: If any player drops a live cup, it is automatically eliminate.
  13. No rearrange on balls back: Cannot rearrange the cups on Balls back. Pretty self explanetory ^^
  14. One rearrange per game: You have one rearrrange per game, ONLY.
  15. Kings start: The winner's of the previous game will start the next game.
  16. Ring of fire: If you have a hallow cyrcular arrangment without any rearrange, and if you place the ball in the middle of that cyrcle, you will win the game without any redemtion or Gentlemen's.
  17. No interfearance: The defenders cannot interfear with the ball until it has hit a cup, ground or the table.
  18. One clebshot per person: Each player has one sleb shot per game.
  19. BANG rule: The shooter can call BANG when his ball has not yet travelled 2/3 of the table length. If sunk, the defenders take out 2 cups but if missed, the shooter has to take out a cup.
  20. Steven Segal & Jackie Chan: After a shot, if the ball is bounced on a cup and not yet hit the ground; The defenders can call this rule and hit the ball with their hand. If the ball hits the shooters, a cup is eliminated BUT if the ball is caught before hitting the ground and Jackie Chan is said, a cup from the defenders is eliminated.
